Name: Bachelor Program of Communication Technology, Multimedia Communication Major (Chương trình cử nhân ngành Công nghệ Truyền thông, chuyên ngành Truyền thông đa phương tiện)
English Name: Bachelor Program of Communication Technology, Multimedia Communication Major
Description: 1. Training Objectives
General objective:
The objective of the Bachelor of Communication Technology program (BCT) is to train students become experts in the fields of Multimedia Communications in digital era. Students will be equipped with necessary skills and knowledge so that they are able to identify goals, well develop and manage communication plans. Especially, students will study how to use tools/software/AI in data analysis as well as in the process of media production which is compatible with different media platforms. With management knowledge and leadership skill, students will be courageous communicators who have large influence and capability to contribute to Communication Industry in Vietnam; to create a foundation for continuously studying and researching in a higher level in communication field. The program includes two majors: Multimedia Communications and Public Relations.
BCT_MC program trains students to become specialists in planning, implementing, and evaluating multimedia, multi-platforms communication campaigns which effectively serve communication goals of businesses/organizations. Students will be equipped from basic to in-depth knowledge and skills in communication. They also learn how to operate tools/software/AI in data analysis as well as in media production process which is compatible with different media platforms in order to effectively optimize communication plans. Capstone projects of BCT_MC students will be practical projects from businesses/organizations.
2. Job positions after graduation:
Graduates of the Multimedia communications have various job opportunities such as:
- Specilist/Director of Content Creation;
- Specialist/Director of Communications;
- Specialist of Advertising and Digital Marketing;
- Reporters, editors for television, radio, print newspapers, magazines;
- Multimedia Communications Researcher;
- Being in charge of media startups/agency, media product production;
- Startup CEO in the field of multimedia communications.
- Researcher/ Lecturer: can implement research tasks at research centers, institutes that do research in Multimedia Communications.
